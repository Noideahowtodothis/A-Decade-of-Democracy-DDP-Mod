## How 500 seats are assigned

- The existing shared D’Hondt allocator, `window.allocateDhondtSeats`, is still used.
- Election support is converted into party vote shares as before.
- The allocator now receives `reichstagSeatCount = Q.reichstag_seat_count || 500`.
- `Q.reichstag_seat_count` is initialized to 500.
- Each eligible party receives seats from the D’Hondt quotient loop until all 500 seats are assigned.
- The resulting seat counts are stored in the existing `Q[party + '_r']` fields.
- Vote-share display fields remain vote shares; coalition mechanics use seat-count fields.

## How to reach coalition formation in-game

1. Play until the Reichstag election event resolves.
2. On the election results screen, review the table showing vote share, seats, and seat share.
3. Continue to the coalition menu.
4. Select **Negotiate a custom coalition**.
5. The coalition formation screen starts with DDP as the anchor party and shows:
   - DDP seats,
   - current coalition parties,
   - coalition seat total out of 500,
   - majority threshold of 251,
   - available parties to negotiate with and their seat counts.
6. Select a party to open its concession screen.
7. Select concessions until selected points meet or exceed required points.
8. Accept the concessions to add that party to the coalition.
9. Repeat until the coalition has at least 251 seats.
10. Click **Form the negotiated coalition government** to continue to ministry selection.

## How to test SPD/DNVP incompatibility

1. Reach **Negotiate a custom coalition** after an election.
2. Negotiate with **DNVP** first.
3. Select enough DNVP concessions to meet the required points.
4. Accept DNVP into the coalition.
5. Return to party selection.
6. Confirm that **SPD** is unavailable and shows an incompatibility reason.
7. Start another election/formation path or test save.
8. Negotiate with **SPD** first and accept SPD into the coalition.
9. Confirm that **DNVP** is unavailable and shows an incompatibility reason.

## How ministry picks are calculated

- The total ministry count is currently hard-coded as 8.
- When the player clicks **Form the negotiated coalition government**, the DDP ministry-pick allowance is calculated as:

```text
ddp_ministry_picks = max(1, round(total_ministries * ddp_seats / coalition_total_seats))
```

- The result is stored in `Q.ddp_ministry_picks` and copied into `Q.leverage` for the existing ministry screen.
- Each ministry selected by the player consumes one pick.
- When the player finishes ministry selection, any remaining unassigned ministries are assigned to non-DDP coalition partners sorted by seat count, cycling through partners from largest to smallest.
